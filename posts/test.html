Python and the principle of Least Astonishment

Written on Saturday, July 9, 2011

When you use something for a long time you will develop some kind of sensing of what goes together and what does not appear to fit the common pattern. The Python community seems to have given this effect a name: if something matches the common patterns it's “pythonic” if it's not, it's deemed “unpythonic”.
